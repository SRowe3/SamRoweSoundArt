About

I am a sound artist and composer currently based around leicester and Bath. My work often takes the form of sonic instullations with a focus on the use and creation of generative and long-duration processes to create sound enviroments. My music largely consists of electronic ambient work inspired by Brian Eno, Aphex Twin, Boards of Canada etc.
