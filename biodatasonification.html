Snake Plant Etude

"In C"Quartet

While thinking of how the plants could be used I had the idea of using them to play Terry Ryles "In C". There are a few reasons for this being a good first experiment. The piece is designed such that each elemet works with every other element withouth explicet communication between "performers", the piece is supposed to have a "random" or at least "psudo-random" element to it and I could essentialy recycle code from my first experiment as , once agin, the plant would just be acting as a random trigger to select a new section. While this is a bit of a tangent and might not further the actual sonification I think it works well and blends well with the philosiphy of Terry Ryley. More infomation is available here
